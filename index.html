VISIT THEATER

This ancient city in Nigeria is fast attaining urbanisation and civilisation as it houses all of the fun cinemas to visit in Oyo state. Living in Ibadan can be really pocket-friendly as it only costs a few naira to treat yourself to some of the latest box office and blockbuster movies.

VISIT SPORT STADIUM

Adamasingba Stadium ( also called Lekan Salami stadium) is a multi-use stadium in Ibadan, Nigeria. It is currently used mostly for football matches and is the home stadium of Shooting Stars FC and other local teams located in Ibadan. The stadium has a capacity of 10,000 people.
